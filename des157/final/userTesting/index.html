The 1980's

Swimsuits were all about making a statement in the 1990’s--Cheetah print was a popular motif. High cut, bright colors were in, from Baywatch red to sunshine yellow, neon pink and tangy orange. As Cosmopolitan said, bright colors blazed brightly against the sea, sky, and even your tan. Angular cuts, frills, stripes and crochet often appeared on swimsuits, and rollerblading was the hit activity!

Throwback to 90's Fashion

Sweatshirts: Disney Style

Everyone has their favorite sweatshirt, whether it earned that title for the road trip you took it on, the inner material (which is the softest texture ever), or maybe it’s your favorite color! My favorite sweatshirts are all Disney themed… because growing up, my family used to go to Disneyland every year and visit our cousin. I loved finding my favorite characters on the sweatshirts, like Tigger and Mickey Mouse… or unique designs and characters, like my Lady and the Tramp sweatshirt.
