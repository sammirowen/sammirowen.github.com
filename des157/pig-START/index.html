Game of PIG

How To Play

There are two players. The player who goes first is randomly chosen, and they roll the dice first.The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a ‘one.’ If a ‘one’ is rolled, this player's turn is over. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a ‘one’ was not rolled) or they can pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

But be aware, if your dice both show “one” (snake eyes), your current score starts at 0 again!
