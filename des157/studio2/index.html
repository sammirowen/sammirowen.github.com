The 90's were full of patterns! From polka dots to plaid, checkers and geometric shapes. But what made the patterns even more fun and interesting were the colors they appeared with! From monochromatic to primary, and even neon, there was nothing too "uncool" to wear!

If you lived through the 90s, you likely owned overalls. And while denim was all the rage, patterned overalls were making a statement themselves. They were a comfortable staple, and often worn with a turtleneck under.

But children weren't the only ones who rocked overalls, they came in all different lengths: shorts, capris, and pants! People didn't forget to wear them with iconic socks and sandals. They could have cool patterns themselves, or be the catalyst for a groovy shirt.
